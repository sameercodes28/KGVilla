# Swedish Building Codes & Regulations Reference

This document provides authoritative references for Swedish building codes and regulations that govern the construction costs calculated in the KGVilla application.

---

## General Building Regulations

### BBR - Boverket's Building Regulations
**Authority**: Boverket (Swedish National Board of Housing, Building and Planning)

**Current Status (2024-2025)**:
- **Active Regulations**: BFS 2011:6 (with amendments) through June 30, 2025
- **New Regulations**: Published December 2024, effective July 1, 2025
- **Transition Period**: One year (until June 30, 2026)

**Key Areas**:
- Structural requirements
- Fire safety
- Energy performance
- Accessibility
- Moisture protection
- Ventilation requirements

**Reference**: [boverket.se](https://www.boverket.se/sv/PBL-kunskapsbanken/regler-om-byggande/boverkets-byggregler/)

## Cost Category References

### 1. Markarbeten (Ground Works)
**Governing Regulations**:
- **BBR Chapter 6**: Foundation and ground requirements
- **Due Diligence Regulations**: BFS 2024:4 (effective January 1, 2025)

**Requirements**:
- Soil investigation and assessment
- Proper drainage systems
- Foundation depth and load-bearing capacity
- Environmental protection during excavation

**Typical Costs Include**:
- Excavation and grading
- Foundation preparation
- Drainage installation
- Site preparation

---

### 2. Stomme & Tak (Structure & Roof)
**Governing Regulations**:
- **BBR Chapter 6**: Structural design and safety
- **Eurocode Standards**: EN 1990-1999 (structural design)
- **Swedish Adaptation**: EKS (European Construction Standards)

**Requirements**:
- Load-bearing capacity calculations
- Wind and snow load resistance
- Structural integrity
- Material specifications

**Typical Costs Include**:
- Concrete foundation and slab
- Timber or steel framing
- Roof trusses and covering
- Structural connections

---

### 3. El & Belysning (Electrical & Lighting)
**Governing Regulations**:
- **SS 436 40 00**: Swedish Electrical Installation Standard (Edition 4, 2023)
- **Authority**: Elsäkerhetsverket (Swedish National Electrical Safety Board)
- **International Basis**: IEC 60364 series, HD 60364 series

**Key Requirements**:
- **Safety**: Protection against electric shock, fire, and burns
- **RCD Protection**: Residual current devices for all circuits
- **Arc Fault Detection**: Required in certain installations
- **EV Charging**: Provisions for electric vehicle charging
- **Solar Installations**: Updated requirements for PV systems
- **Medical Areas**: Special requirements for healthcare facilities

**Certification**:
- All electrical work must be performed by authorized electricians
- Installation must comply with Elsäkerhetsverket regulations
- Certification required upon completion

**Reference**: [elstandard.se](https://www.elstandard.se/), [elsakerhetsverket.se](https://www.elsakerhetsverket.se/)

**Typical Costs Include**:
- Main electrical panel and distribution
- Wiring and conduits
- Outlets and switches
- Lighting fixtures
- Safety devices (RCDs, circuit breakers)

---

### 4. VVS (Plumbing, Heating, Ventilation)
**Governing Regulations**:
- **Säker Vatteninstallation 2021:1**: Safe Water Installation regulations
- **Next Edition**: 2026:1 (effective January 1, 2026)
- **Authority**: Säker Vatten AB (industry organization)
- **Compliance**: Boverket BBR + Consumer Services Act

**Key Requirements**:
- **Water Damage Prevention**: Proper installation to prevent leaks
- **Legionella Protection**: Temperature controls and circulation
- **Burn Prevention**: Thermostatic mixing valves
- **Backflow Prevention**: Check valves and air gaps
- **Pressure Testing**: Mandatory for all water and heating installations
- **Waterproofing**: Coordination with wet room standards

**Certification**:
- Only authorized VVS companies can issue Säker Vatten certificates
- Personnel must have industry legitimation (diplomas)
- Company must have liability insurance and F-tax registration

**Reference**: [sakervatten.se](https://www.sakervatten.se/)

**Typical Costs Include**:
- Water supply piping
- Drainage systems
- Heating system (radiators, underfloor heating)
- Ventilation ductwork and fans
- Bathroom fixtures
- Kitchen plumbing

---

### 5. Innerväggar & Isolering (Interior Walls & Insulation)
**Governing Regulations**:
- **BBR Chapter 9**: Energy management and insulation
- **EU Directive**: EPBD (Energy Performance of Buildings Directive)

**Requirements**:
- Minimum U-values for walls, floors, and roofs
- Thermal bridge prevention
- Airtightness standards
- Sound insulation between units

**Typical Costs Include**:
- Interior wall framing
- Insulation materials
- Vapor barriers
- Drywall/plasterboard

---

### 6. Golv & Kakel (Flooring & Tiles)
**Governing Regulations**:
- **Säker Vatten**: Wet room waterproofing requirements
- **Industry Standards**: 
  - Byggkeramikrådet (ceramic tiles)
  - Golvbranschens Våtrumskontroll (wet room flooring)

**Requirements**:
- Waterproofing in wet areas (bathrooms, laundry)
- Floor drains and slopes
- Proper substrate preparation
- Tile installation standards

**Typical Costs Include**:
- Flooring materials (wood, tile, vinyl)
- Waterproofing membranes
- Tile adhesive and grout
- Floor leveling compounds

---

### 7. Målning & Finish (Painting & Finishing)
**Governing Regulations**:
- **BBR**: General finish requirements
- **Måleribranschens Våtrumskontroll**: Wet room painting standards
- **Environmental Regulations**: VOC limits for paints

**Requirements**:
- Moisture-resistant materials in wet areas
- Proper surface preparation
- Environmental compliance

**Typical Costs Include**:
- Interior painting
- Trim and molding
- Door and window finishing
- Final cleaning

---

### 8. Byggherrekostnader (Client/Owner Costs)
**Governing Regulations**:
- **PBL**: Planning and Building Act
- **PBF**: Planning and Building Ordinance

**Requirements**:
- Building permits and inspections
- Professional consultations (architect, engineer)
- Insurance and warranties
- Final inspections and approvals

**Typical Costs Include**:
- Building permit fees
- Architect and engineer fees
- Surveying and soil investigation
- Construction insurance
- Final inspection fees
- Connection fees (water, sewer, electricity)

## Energy Performance Requirements

**BBR Chapter 9** (Updated regulations coming 2025):
- **Energy Performance Certificate**: Required for all new buildings
- **Primary Energy**: Maximum limits based on building type
- **U-values**: Specific requirements for building envelope
- **Ventilation**: Minimum air change rates
- **Heating Systems**: Efficiency requirements

**EU Alignment**:
- Coordinated with EPBD (Energy Performance of Buildings Directive)
- New regulations expected to harmonize with EU standards

## Quality Assurance & Certification

### Required Certifications
1. **Electrical**: Elsäkerhetsverket compliance certificate
2. **Plumbing**: Säker Vatten installation certificate
3. **Energy**: Energy Performance Certificate (EPC)
4. **Final Inspection**: Municipal building inspector approval

### Insurance Requirements
- Most Swedish insurance companies require compliance with:
  - Säker Vatten for plumbing
  - SS 436 40 00 for electrical
  - BBR for general construction
- Non-compliance may void insurance coverage for water damage or electrical fires

## Cost Calculation Basis

The costs in the KGVilla application are based on:
1. **2024 Swedish Market Prices**: Current material and labor costs
2. **Regulatory Compliance**: All costs include compliance with above standards
3. **Professional Installation**: Assumes work by authorized/certified contractors
4. **Quality Materials**: Mid-to-high range materials meeting Swedish standards
5. **Regional Variations**: Prices may vary by location in Sweden

---

## Important Notes

### Transition Period (2024-2026)
- Projects started before July 1, 2025 can use old BBR regulations
- Projects started after July 1, 2025 must use new regulations
- Cannot mix old and new regulations within a single project
- Transition period ends June 30, 2026

### Professional Requirements
- **Electrical Work**: Must be performed by authorized electricians
- **Plumbing Work**: Must be performed by authorized VVS companies
- **Structural Work**: May require certified structural engineer approval
- **Building Permits**: Required for most construction work
