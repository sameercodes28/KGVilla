# SS 436 40 00 (Electrical) - Why it matters for Villas

**What is it?**
The standard for electrical installation. It defines safety "Zones" in bathrooms where electricity is fatal.

**Relevance to KGVilla:**
*   **Safety:** We must price higher IP-rated (waterproof) sockets for bathrooms.
*   **Capacity:** Modern villas need heavy feeds for Induction Hobs and EV Chargers. Our AI assumes these heavy cables are required.

**Key AI Check:** "Bathroom Zone 1? No sockets allowed."
